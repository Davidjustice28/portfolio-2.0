About Me

Before web development, most of my career has been in various sales roles including a car salesman for and most recently a real estate agent. I also served a short term in the U.S Army as a field artillery tactical data systems specialists.

My first introduction to programming came along when I bought my wife a book on programming so that she could learn for work. I was curious one day and read a few pages. Before you know it I was hooked. I completed a course called "Python For Everyone" and immediately knew that coding was for me. Being in sales for so long, I know first hand how much a great website or application can do for a product so I decided to pursue web development. Through hours of project-based learning and online courses like The Odin Project and FreeCodeCamp, I've managed to self-teach myself how to develop dynamic websites based around the Javascript language.
